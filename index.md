Hi there! My name is Bram Rodenburg. I am currently a Data Engineer in the consulting branch of <a href="https://www.lynxanalytics.com/" target="_blank">Lynx Analytics</a>.
Since August 2019 I'm based in Singapore. Before that I was spending my time for Lynx between Germany, Hungary and The Netherlands.
I primarily building data and machine learning pipelines using Spark for projects across several industries (banking, retail and telecom).

Before Lynx I was a student at the <a href="https://www.vu.nl">Vrije Universiteit (VU)</a> in Amsterdam.
I wrote my Master Thesis at <a href="https://www.nki.nl">the Netherlands Cancer Institute (NKI)</a> on deep learning in immunofluorescence imaging. 
I developed a pipeline to process multispectral images of head and neck carcinomas that could distinguish malignant from normal epithelium. 
I was supervised by Ted Meeds and Sanne Abeln from the VU Bioinformatics department, and Christian Rausch and Erik Hooijberg from the NKI.

During my studies, I worked as a teaching assistant (TA) for the following courses:
- Advanced Programming (Java)
- Introduction to Computer Science
- Introduction to Programming (Python)
- Programming (Java)
- Project Application Development

Besides working as a TA, I also worked as a part-time software developer for NICE Systems. I was mainly involved in small research projects. One of these projects was about language detection in audio.
